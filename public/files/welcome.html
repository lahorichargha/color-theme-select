(color-theme-select)
Interactive color-theme preview and generator.

color-theme is an emacs-lisp mode for skinning your emacs.

This site will help you create your own color themes or preview and customize popular ones.

Let every Jedi of the emacs order choose the colors of his lightsaber.



* Usage:
As you can see, this site's GUI mimics an emacs window showing four buffers:
* Color Theme Selection The upper right buffer lists known color themes.
* Customize Face The upper left buffer for customizing most face attributes.
* Speedbar Shows a list of htmlized files intended for theme preview.
* Mode Preview Holds content for htmlized files.

Click on any name from the Color Theme Selection buffer. Once loaded, you can customize every face defined by the theme in the Customize Face buffer. Open some files from the Speedbar buffer to see how they would look with your current theme.

When you are happy with your settings, set a name for your theme in the minibuffer prompt, click the Save button and install it on your .emacs file.

This site generates a permalink for each theme you save, allowing you to share them with friends and contribute them for being listed here.

Themes are saved as gists which means they can be reloaded at any time, just give the gist-id to this site after the hash like this: #GIST
